Bringing Retro Back

We aspire to make retro games with a refreshing twist. We were established in 2016 by a couple of entrepreneurial gamers who want to give long time gamers a sense of nostalgia and new gamers a taste of the past.

Join our Family

We’re looking for humble but ambitious, retro-sharp professionals who can teach us a thing or two. We promise to return the favor. These individuals will become part of entrepreneurial teams who prefer action (kicking ass) over process and bureaucracy (taking names). We embrace those who see things differently, who aren't afraid to experiment, and who have a healthy disregard for the rules.

Applicants should first learn about Neoretro and play Quadrix because we're looking for people who, like us, take play seriously and for whom it's never "just a game." We play lots of games, especially our own, because hands-on gaming experience is our ultimate guiding compass.
